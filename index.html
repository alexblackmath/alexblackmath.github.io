Alexander E. Black

I am a fourth year PhD student at UC Davis in the math department happy to be working with Jesús De Loera. I study the interaction between algebraic and geometric combinatorics and optimization. I'm particularly interested in the behavior of the simplex method. I also perform stand up comedy, but I suspect if you're here, it's because of the math.
